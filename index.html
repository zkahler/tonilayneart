The Artist

About Toni Layne

Toni Layne is a painter whose work is rooted in the textures of everyday life — the interplay of light across a worn surface, the quiet drama of a changing sky, and the stories held in color and form.

With a deep love for oils and watercolors, Toni's work bridges the intimate and the grand. Each piece is built slowly, with intention — layers of paint forming a world that invites the viewer to linger and discover.

Works have been exhibited in regional galleries and collected by private collectors across the United States. Commissions are accepted on a limited basis throughout the year.
